Goal of this study

The goal of this study is to evaluate the notion of Living Research, a toolkit for sharing insights gained from user research by focusing on rich storytelling.

What we want you to do

If you agree to take part in this study, we ask you to join us in a co-designing session that lasts 60 minutes in total. In this session we ask you to take part in a creative problem solving exercise, which is followed by a discussion of the impact that the provided research deliverables have had on your design process. After this session, we will share the results of this discussion with you, plus a questionnaire for evaluation purposes. We kindly request you to take the time to fill out this evaluation.

Confidentiality

All data generated during- and after these sessions will be confidential, and stored securely. In any report that might be published, personally identifiable information will be removed to preserve your privacy. Only the researcher will have access to this data.

Participation is voluntary

Participation in this study is completely voluntary. If you decide to participate, you retain the right to withdraw from this study. If you withdraw, no data will be kept for analysis. If you have any concerns regarding your participation, or have any other questions, you are free to contact the researcher of this study. The performing researcher is Arthur Geel.

  • If you wish to contact an independent party, you may contact Dr. Ir. Javed Khan, through v.j.khan@tue.nl.

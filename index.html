About Lucas

Lucas has a busy life . Between his nine-to-five job and the ongoing renovations in his house, he does not have a lot of free time to work with. He won't settle for beers that simply are 'okay' or ones he has drank plenty of times — he's actively searching for fresh, new ales to try.

Behavioural Considerations

  • Expects the site experience to reflect the business's culture and values.
  • Sometimes has guests over, should also have some 'mainstream' brews that 'non-connaisseurs' may appreciate.
  • Thoroughly compares multiple breweries with similar products.

Frustrations

  • Thinks that the mainstream production of special beers devalue the range of available beers. Big breweries cater to the common denominator, innovation in brewing hardly happens.
  • Has no time for mediocre beers, as he only has limited opportunities to engage in tasting sessions with his peers.

Goals

  • Needs to see reasons why the beers are interesting to him: have they won awards, had good PR, gets the community interested?
  • Wants to save the hassle of endlessly browsing with minimal results. Expects to receive a quality product every time.

Tasks

  • Explore different beers and see how much they cost.
  • Read about the brand's culture, identity and employees: who actually create the beers, and why should he care?
  • Purchase beers efficiently, and have a fast shipping- or pickup fulfilment system.
